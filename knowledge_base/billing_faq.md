# Billing FAQ

## Accepted payment methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We do not accept checks or bank transfers for standard orders.

## When am I charged?

Your card is authorized at checkout and charged when the order ships. Pre-orders are charged at the time of order.

## Subscription billing

CloudCart Pro subscriptions bill monthly on the date you signed up. You can switch to annual billing (2 months free) from Account → Billing. Cancel anytime; access continues until the end of the paid period.

## Duplicate or incorrect charges

If you see a duplicate charge, it is usually a temporary authorization hold that disappears within 3-5 business days. If a charge is still duplicated after 5 days, contact support with the last 4 digits of the card and the charge dates - never share the full card number.

## Invoices and receipts

Receipts are emailed automatically after every charge. Business customers can download tax invoices from Account → Billing → Invoice History.
