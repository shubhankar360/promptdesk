# Account Management

## Resetting your password

Click "Forgot password" on the login page and enter your account email. A reset link valid for 30 minutes will be sent. If it doesn't arrive, check spam and confirm you used the email on the account.

## Changing your email address

Go to Account → Profile → Email and enter the new address. A confirmation link is sent to both the old and new email for security.

## Two-factor authentication

Enable 2FA under Account → Security. We support authenticator apps and SMS. Recovery codes are shown once - store them safely.

## Suspected unauthorized access

If you believe your account was accessed without permission, reset your password immediately and contact support. Our security team reviews access logs and can lock the account while investigating. These cases are always handled by a human agent.

## Deleting your account

Account deletion is permanent and removes order history, saved addresses, and subscriptions. Request deletion from Account → Privacy → Delete Account; a human agent confirms the request within 48 hours.
