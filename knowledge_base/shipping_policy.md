# Shipping Policy

## Standard shipping

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the continental US and costs $4.99. Orders over $50 ship free. Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of dispatch.

## Express shipping

Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and costs $14.99. Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same day. Express is not available for PO boxes.

## International shipping

We ship to Canada, the UK, EU, Australia, and India. International delivery takes 7-21 business days depending on destination and customs. Duties and taxes are the customer's responsibility and are not included at checkout.

## Lost or delayed packages

If a package has not arrived 3 business days after the estimated delivery date, contact support with your order number. We file a carrier investigation and either reship or refund within 5 business days of the investigation opening.
