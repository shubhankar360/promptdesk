# Returns Policy

## Return window

Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Final-sale items and gift cards cannot be returned.

## How to start a return

Log in to your CloudCart account, open Orders, select the item, and click "Start Return". You'll receive a prepaid shipping label by email. Drop the package at any carrier location.

## Refund timing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days after we receive the returned item. You'll get an email confirmation when the refund is processed.

## Damaged or wrong items

If your item arrived damaged or you received the wrong item, you do not need to return it in some cases. Contact support with a photo within 7 days of delivery and we will send a replacement or refund immediately.

## Exchanges

We don't offer direct exchanges. Return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the item you want.
